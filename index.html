Common questions

Frequently asked.

Will my patients know it's AI?
No. We use the most advanced voice technology available — your callers will think they're talking to a polished, professional receptionist. We've tested this on hundreds of callers and the average person doesn't notice.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
It politely takes a message with the caller's name, number, and question, and texts you immediately. You can call them back at your convenience. Nothing is ever missed.
Will it work with my existing phone number?
Yes. We forward your existing number through our system. Your patients call the same number they always have. You don't need to change anything on your end.
Can it actually book into my real calendar?
Yes. We integrate with Dentrix, Open Dental, NexHealth, Calendly, Google Calendar, and most modern practice management systems.
What if I need to make changes after launch?
Minor tweaks (changing hours, adding services, updating prices) are included in your monthly fee. Major rebuilds are quoted separately.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Month-to-month after setup. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
How is this different from a regular answering service?
Answering services use humans and cost $1,500-$3,000/month for limited hours. They don't book appointments, don't speak fluent Spanish, and miss calls during peak times. Our AI answers every call 24/7, books directly, and costs a fraction of the price.
What if it doesn't work for my practice?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the monthly fee. If your AI receptionist doesn't reduce your missed calls in the first 30 days, we refund. No questions asked.
